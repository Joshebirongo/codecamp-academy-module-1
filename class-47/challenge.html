Movie ticket Discount


Description

Create a program that asks the user to enter their age and determines the type of movie ticket discount they receive.


Rules

Use following conditions:

  • If the person is 12 years old or younger → they receive a Child Discount
  • If the person is between 13 and 59 years old → they pay a Regular Ticket
  • If the person is 60 years old or older → they receive a Senior Discount
